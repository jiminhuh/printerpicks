## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

This site is operated as an independent review platform. This page explains how we handle your data.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect anonymous usage data:

**Google Analytics** tracks anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, device type, and general location. This helps us understand which content is useful. Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

**Google AdSense** may display advertisements on our site and use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

## Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Our site uses cookies through Google Analytics (traffic analysis) and Google AdSense (ad personalization). Most browsers let you refuse cookies or alert you when one is being sent.

## Affiliate Links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate partners may use cookies to track referrals. Our affiliate relationships never influence our reviews or recommendations.

## Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites that we do not operate. We have no control over their content or privacy practices.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time by posting the new version on this page.
